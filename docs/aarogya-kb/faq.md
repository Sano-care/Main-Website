## About Sanocare

**Q: Who is Sanocare?**
Sanocare Tech Innovations Pvt. Ltd. is a home healthcare service across Delhi NCR. A trained Sanocare Medic visits your home for the physical care; an MBBS doctor consults virtually (call/video) to diagnose and prescribe. We also do lab sample collection at home and pure teleconsultation.

**Q: Are your staff qualified?**
Yes. Doctors are MBBS-qualified and registered with the relevant medical council (they consult on live video, they do not visit in person). Medics hold GNM or B.Sc Nursing qualifications. Phlebotomists are certified. Every staff member is background-verified and Sanocare-employed — we don't subcontract.

## About Services

**Q: Which areas do you cover?**
Delhi NCR — Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, Ghaziabad, Faridabad (in-person). Teleconsultation is available anywhere in India. Share your live location and we'll confirm coverage.

**Q: How quickly can you send someone?**
- Home Visit + Doctor Consult: a Medic reaches you within 30 minutes of confirmation; the doctor consults on video right after.
- Home Nursing: a Medic within 30 minutes.
- Teleconsultation: a doctor calls within 15 minutes.
- Lab Test at Home: morning (7–10 AM) or evening (5–8 PM) collection slots; reports in 24–48 hours.

**Q: What are your hours?**
Standard hours are 9 AM – 9 PM. Extended care is available on request — message us and we'll confirm coverage.

**Q: Can I book for someone other than myself?**
Yes — most patients book for parents, spouses, or children. We just need patient name, age, location, and your relationship to them.

## About Payment

**Q: How do I pay?**
The Medic collects at your doorstep — UPI, QR, and cash are all accepted. There is no payment on chat.

**Q: Can you send me a payment link?**
No — Sanocare never sends payment links and never asks for card, UPI PIN, or OTP over WhatsApp. The Medic collects at the door.

**Q: What's your refund policy?**
You can cancel free of charge any time before the Medic has departed for your home. Once the visit is completed, it's charged in full. If we fail to deliver the service, it's a full refund. For a billing dispute, dial +91 97119 77782.
